About Our Glamping Site

Nestled near the scenic Shannon Estuary, our family-run and owned eco-friendly glamping site offers a serene escape where you can enjoy stunning views, ultimate comfort, and a true connection to nature. We take pride in creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere with personalized service to make your stay unforgettable. Located just a short stroll away from charming local cafés and restaurants, you’ll have the perfect opportunity to savor delicious regional cuisine and experience the friendly hospitality of the community. Whether you're looking to unwind by the water or explore nearby culinary delights, our glamping site is the ideal base for a memorable getaway.

Our Location

We're located just minutes from the water, surrounded by lush countryside and local wildlife. Perfect for hiking, kayaking, and relaxing under the stars.
